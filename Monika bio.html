Monika Marchewka

Monika Marchewka (b. 1988), is a Polish artist based in Gdynia, Poland. She graduated from the Jan Matejko Academy of Fine Arts in Krakow in 2013. After her studies, she worked as an animator on "Loving Vincent" (2017) and "The Peasants" (2023). Her artworks are known for dreamlike imagery, often featuring elements like pearls, tears, and mist, creating immersive, fantastical worlds. Drawing on her film background, her paintings are cinematic, with detailed close-ups and visual storytelling. Monika has exhibited internationally, including solo shows such as "Written off" in 2016 and "No Regrets" in 2023.
